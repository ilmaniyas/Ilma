Do you believe that a book can change your life?
In 2015, Ilma Niyas (Chief Bookaholic) accidentally landed on a book called The 4-Hour Work Week by Tim Ferriss. This book led him to start a blog. That blog led him to start an online bookstore. That bookstore is called Hiddenbooks.lk So what do you think? Do you think a book can change your life?
We definitely think so.

We are on a mission to bring down the world’s best books that can change your life. Our book collection is curated by a Bookaholic who is ready to eat books if that’ll change his life. That’s how much we believe in the power of books.
